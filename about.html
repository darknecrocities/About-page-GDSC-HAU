What are the Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC)?Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are university-based community clubs for students interested in technology, powered by Google Developers. The goal is to assist students in bridging the theoretical and practical barrier.
Who are the Google Developer Student Clubs - Holy Angel University?
We are a group of students with a strong interest in technology. Our mission is to bring students from all backgrounds together who enjoy studying and applying their knowledge to real-world problems.
Why join Google Developer Student Club - Holy Angel University?The Google Developer Student Clubs - Holy Angel University, which was founded in 2021, organizes and facilitates workshops, hackathons, speaker sessions, and Study Jams to help students develop technological abilities. By becoming a member, you will be able to:
01
Enhance your technical abilities in a peer-to-peer learning environment collaborate and use what you've learned by assisting in the creation of community solutions;
02
Take part in the annual Solution Challenge competition for a chance to win Google prizes.
03
Meet tech executives and professionals to broaden your network.
04
Have a good time and meet new people who are interested in developer technology.
05
Workshops, webinars, hackathons, and other fascinating activities are available to attend;
How to be part of Google Developer Student Clubs - Holy Angel University?Every student with a desire in becoming a developer, regardless of degree, is welcome. The sole criterion is that you have a strong interest in technology.
